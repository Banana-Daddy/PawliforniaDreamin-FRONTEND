An introduction

Irreplaceable memories are guaranteed when our team takes the leash. Pawlifornia Dreamin' is an event pet care service built on one belief — that your four-legged best friend belongs in the frame, the ceremony, and the story. We coordinate the details so you don't have to. Pickup, transport, event attire, vendor coordination, and quality insured care from first kiss to last dance.

№ 06 Behind the Leash

Founded by Rhoda Coscetti — Coachella Valley native, UC Davis Animal Biology, eight years in animal welfare across rescues and municipal shelters. Rhoda built Pawlifornia Dreamin' on a simple idea: couples shouldn't have to choose between a polished celebration and including the pet who started the family. She trains every handler on the team.
